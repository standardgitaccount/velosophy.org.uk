Our Methodology

This section outlines the systematic approach used to develop the reports. It highlights the key stages from planning to publication, ensuring thorough research and effective collaboration among committee members to produce comprehensive and reliable outcomes.

01

Planning

Defining the purpose and scope, establishing aims and objectives, and conducting preliminary research.

02

Research

Verifying sources for trustworthiness using a CRAAP analysis and beginning to draft notes.

03

Drafting

Drafting, iterating, and developing the layout of the report to bring all elements together.

04

Finalizing

The report is sent to all committee members for review, during which final amendments are made.

05

Publication

The report is published, either as a complimentary resource or available for a nominal fee.
